About the association

Founded in 2006, Academia Europa brings together professionals and researchers committed to advancing knowledge through interdisciplinary exchange. Our publications, events, and working groups connect members across institutions and borders.

Our mission

To facilitate rigorous academic exchange and support members at every stage of their careers through publications, events, and collaborative networks.

Membership

Open to researchers, professionals and graduate students. Members gain access to publications, discounted event registration, and our working groups.

Publications

Our articles, working papers and policy briefs are freely accessible to all. Members may submit for review by the editorial board.

Contact

General enquiries: info@academiaeuropa.org
Submissions: submissions@academiaeuropa.org
